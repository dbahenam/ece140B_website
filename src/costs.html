The Costs

The price of the raw material will total around 35$ not including the costs of hosting the pi server and the database server. If we add the costs of hosting the servers, our total price will be an extra $5 a month. We plan on selling 500 units a month at a price of $65 per unit to be able to negate the monthly costs of hosting the servers. Our manufacturing and labor for our product will be done by us so we will hand build the devices as a starting point. Therefore our manufacturing/labor costs will be zero.
